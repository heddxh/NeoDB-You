<p><i>NeoDB You</i> is a native Android app for NeoDB, crafted with Jetpack Compose and Material
    3/You.</p><p><br><b>Features</b></p>
<ul>
    <li>Material 3/You design</li>
    <li>Native UI</li>
    <li>Login into any NeoDB servers</li>
    <li>View trending entries</li>
    <li>Search with keywords</li>
    <li>Access detailed information and reviews</li>
    <li>Compose and modify your own reviews</li>
    <li>Visualize your library with heat map.</li>
</ul><p>More features on the way...</p>
